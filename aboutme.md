**MOKUKU** is a small team focused on building creative hardware and software that makes technology more fun, expressive, and interactive.

Our products combine **embedded systems, custom displays, Bluetooth connectivity, and open software tools** to create devices that are both practical and playful. We believe technology should not only be functional, but also **engaging and customizable**.

MOKUKU devices are designed to be:

* **Developer-friendly** – with open protocols and example code
* **Customizable** – allowing users to create their own display layouts and interactions
* **Playful and expressive** – bringing personality to everyday devices

We actively support developers and makers by sharing **open documentation, BLE protocols, and example applications**, so the community can build their own ideas on top of the platform.

Whether you're a **developer, maker, or hardware enthusiast**, MOKUKU aims to provide tools that make experimentation easy and fun.
